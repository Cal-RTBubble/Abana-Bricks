About Abana Bricks

Abana Bricks is a growing real-estate startup created with one goal - to make renting homes easier, faster, and more comfortable for everyone. We connect tenants with well-maintained homes, trusted owners, and flexible leasing options. Whether you are searching for a family home, bachelor accommodation, or a short-term stay, we make the journey smooth from start to finish.

Vision

Our vision is to create a hassle-free renting experience for everyone. We aim to build a system where booking a rental home is as easy as booking a hotel-fast, transparent, and online.
